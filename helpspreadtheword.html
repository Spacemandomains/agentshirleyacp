Help Spread the Word

The @FCC launched a new program on December 31, 2021: Low-income households can now apply for the Affordable Connectivity Program, which includes monthly internet discounts, an Enhanced Tribal Benefit, and a one-time device discount. To learn more, go to AffordableConnectivity.gov. (Twitter)

USAC has created a toolkit that includes resources organizations can use to make consumers aware of the new Affordable Connectivity Program and give them the opportunity to participate.
